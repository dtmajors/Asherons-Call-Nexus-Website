Server Systems & Features

Progression & Rates

  • Base XP reduced by 50% server‑wide; Quest XP & Luminance reduced by 20%.
  • Level cap: 275; Force Materialize: 6s on login/portal.

Economy & Casino

  • Uber-Stakes Gambling Gems: High-risk currency for the PvP-only Vault.
  • The Pit Boss: Trades Uber-Stakes Gems for a roll at top-tier rewards (PvP-only Den).
  • The Forge of Souls: 50/50 wager of XP or Luminance — double on win, lose the offering on loss.
  • Danky the Terrible & other gamblers: Coin flip for 5 MMDs to try to double; additional games available.
  • Wheel of Fortune: Daily spin (20-hour timer) for level 150+.
  • The Jester: Non-PK trade — 1 Uber-Stakes Gem → 2 Nexus Tokens.
  • Forgemaster of Souls: 50/50 double‑or‑lose (XP 100M/500M/1B or LUM 50k/100k/150k). 12h cooldown.

PK / Non‑PK

  • Toggle PK with Bael’Zharon (30‑min)
  • Asheron (60 Nexus Tokens) or Emissary (free, every 3 days).

Unique Gameplay Systems

Arena System

  • Event Types: 1v1, 2v2, Free‑For‑All (FFA), and Group matches
  • Smart Matchmaking: respects level tiers; prioritizes existing fellowships for team events
  • ELO Ranking: server‑wide rating based on match performance
  • Observer Mode: watch live matches safely and invisibly
  • Rotating Maps: fight in Icebox, The Drowned Pit, Gladiator’s Arena, and more

Dynamic Invasions

  • World Events: killing mobs may trigger massive invasions in one of 17 towns
  • Mini‑Bosses: powerful bosses spawn that require players to band together
  • Valuable Tokens: bosses drop limited tokens redeemable with the Clerk of Nexus Deeds (Simiran)

Town Control

  • Allegiance‑based captures with zerg limit caps (max 5 allegiances)
  • Rewards: controlled towns yield a steady income of Nexus Tokens for the allegiance
  • No Vendors: town‑control vendors removed to balance the economy

Progression System

  • XP Rate: XP reduced by 50% for slower, meaningful progression
  • Percentage‑Based Rewards: most quests scale XP rewards by level
  • Max Level: 275
  • Starter Bonuses: 96‑slot backpack and Throne of Destiny Pre‑Order gem on creation
  • Retail Feel: retail casting behavior; improved melee and archery
  • Force Materialization: 6s on login/portal
  • Vitae: max penalty capped at 20%
  • PvP Adjustments: cleaving disabled; rare gems cannot be used in PvP; consumable chug rate reduced
  • Tinkering Lotto: randomized chances for enhanced results while tinkering
  • Account Policy: max 5 accounts per IP; 15 characters per account; VPNs disabled (exceptions possible)

Nexus Server Rules & Conduct Policy

Welcome to Nexus, a custom White Server and Red Server experience. Each server path offers unique custom content. For more details, please refer to the promotional video in the announcements channel.

Any serious inquiries should be sent through the #support-tickets channel where a ticket will be generated and sent to the Nexus Admin Team.

Limits & Enforcement

  • IP Limit: A maximum of 5 accounts per IP address logged in at one time. VPNs or other methods to bypass this limit are prohibited. Exceptions may be granted under specific circumstances.
  • Character Limit: Up to 15 characters per account.
  • Enforcement: All penalties are time-limited and subject to administrative review. Captain has final authority.

Conduct Violations

The following actions are prohibited and will result in disciplinary measures:

  • Use of PvP macros
  • Exploiting metas in custom content areas or PvP dungeons
  • Excessive harassment or personal attacks
  • Use of racial slurs
  • Deliberately inflammatory or disruptive behavior as judged by staff

Exploitation & Cheating

Exploitation of any kind may result in immediate action, bypassing the strike system entirely. This includes but is not limited to the following:

  • Abusing quests, items, or starting zones for exploitative gain. If you are unsure, report it to staff.
  • Using VPNs or other methods to bypass account limits.
  • Utilizing metas in PvP zones, the Colosseum, or Gauntlet. These metas overload the server and are not permitted. Breaking this rule will result in severe punishment.
  • Exploiting the PvE/PvP switching system.
  • Using third-party applications or plugins to cheat or manipulate the client. This includes Blink and Picker. Alpha anti-cheat is active and detection is ongoing.
  • Note: Tools such as War Detect and plugin suites like Defiance or FOG are not policed.

PvP Zone Clarification

No automation may be used when recalling into PvP areas. All entries into PvP areas must be performed manually.

Examples:

  • Player A detects Player B and recalls using automation software – this is a rule break.
  • Player A detects Player B and logs off manually – this is acceptable.
  • Player A logs on PK and automatically recalls into a PvP zone – this is a rule break.
  • Player A logs on PK, manually recalls back into a PvP zone, and sets up macros – this is acceptable.

Do not attempt to bypass systems. If you are AFK in these zones and log on PK, you must manually return. Automation to re-enter or set up macros is prohibited.

Harassment Policy

Harassment of other players or staff, either in-game or on Discord, will not be tolerated. This includes verbal abuse or targeted behavior. All cases will be reviewed individually.

Accusations of cheating must be sent directly to staff with proof. Public accusations without evidence are prohibited. Repeated false accusations will be treated as harassment.

Nepotism claims or insinuations will not be tolerated. If you believe wrongdoing has occurred, contact Captain or another staff member directly. Failure to follow this rule may result in account termination.

Disciplinary Progression

  1. 1st Offense: Verbal or written warning
  2. 2nd Offense: 24-hour mute
  3. 3rd Offense: 24-hour ban
  4. 4th Offense: 1-week ban

Repeat offenses may trigger a Nexus Admin Team vote. Serious cases (illegal activity or disclosure of real-life information) are handled on a case-by-case basis.

Impersonation Rule

Direct impersonation of active or well-known players in the community is prohibited. Stealing names or using deceptive variations is not allowed. This does not apply to old monarch names, which are fair game. Use common sense.

Account Sharing Disclaimer

Sharing your account with other players is strongly discouraged. You assume full responsibility for any consequences. The Nexus Admin Team will not mediate disputes, resolve item loss, or perform rollbacks related to shared accounts.

Final Note

Everyone should be here to have fun. Troublemakers will be dealt with, no exceptions. The rules apply equally to all players. Captain is an active administrator and while bans are never the first choice, do not assume you are not being watched. That would be your first mistake.
